FIFA World Cup 2014 News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the FIFA World Cup qualifiers was recorded on 11 April 2001, when Australia beat American Samoa 31-0. This legendary match also brought global renown for Archie Thompson, whose 13-goal feat set a game world record. And though the defeat wounded American Samoa ignominiously, their inspiring recovery became the subject of the documentary, Next Goal Wins, showing across the world.
